Sung Jin-woo (성진우)

About Sung Jin-woo

Originally an infamous weak E-Rank hunter, he gets the chance of a lifetime when he is selected as the Player of a magical program called the System and gains the unique ability to grow in strength without limit.

Sung-Jin-woo's characteristics

  1. He is humble
  2. He is benevolent individual who cares deeply for his family and has a strong thirst for power.
  3. He is good-hearted and generous to others as well
